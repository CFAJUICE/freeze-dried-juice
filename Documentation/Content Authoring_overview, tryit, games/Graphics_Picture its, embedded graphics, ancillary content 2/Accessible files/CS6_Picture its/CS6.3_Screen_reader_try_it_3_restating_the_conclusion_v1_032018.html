Restating the conclusion

To restate the conclusion, first read the conclusion and identify the author's key ideas. Then explain each idea in your own words.

The conclusion's key ideas

Read the conclusion and decide what the author's key ideas are.

I really recommend that you create a group of interested community members to make a plan. Make sure your group contains some financial and tax experts and representation from your town government. Then, work with local suppliers and state and federal governments to see what is available for your community.
