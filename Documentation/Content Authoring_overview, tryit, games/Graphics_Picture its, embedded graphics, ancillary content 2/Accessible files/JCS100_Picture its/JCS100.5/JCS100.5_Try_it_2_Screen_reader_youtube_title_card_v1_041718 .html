Tips for finding the information you need

A title card displays within the video as soon as it starts playing. The title card often includes the video's title, followed by the name of its creator. The creator's name may be preceded by the words "by" or "with." If the name given on the title card is different from the author's screen name, it will be the author's real name. For this video, the author's real name is Malik Moussa.
