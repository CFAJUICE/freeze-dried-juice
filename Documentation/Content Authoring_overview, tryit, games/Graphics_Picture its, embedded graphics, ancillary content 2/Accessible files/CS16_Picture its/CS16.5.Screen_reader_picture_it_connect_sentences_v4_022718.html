Connect sentences

Use transition words to connect sentences in your paragraph and help your writing flow smoothly. Some transition words show the importance of ideas, while others indicate the order in which things happened.

Showing the importance of ideas

Here is a paragraph with three ideas, but it doesn't use transition words to connect them. Can you tell which step is most important? Will you know if you can skip the last step?

An important part of helping your child with homework is providing a consistent place for them to work. Make sure this space includes a table or desktop large enough for school materials. Make sure your child always works in the same place. The space should be near you so that you are available to answer questions.

It's hard to say which step is most important or if any can be skipped. Including transition words between ideas will show which ideas are most important.

Revised paragraph with transition words

An important part of helping your child with homework is providing a consistent place for them to work. First, make sure this space includes a table or desktop large enough for school materials. Next, make sure your child always works in the same place. Most important, the space should be near you so that you are available to answer questions.

The transition words "first", "next", and "most important" show the importance of ideas. Better not skip the last step; it's the most important!

Showing the order of events

Here is a paragraph describing how to cook rice, but it doesn't use transition words. Do you have to do these steps in a specific order? For example, can you add rice before you boil the water?

Rice is a quick and easy side dish to prepare. Pour two cups of water into a pot and bring it to a boil. Add one cup of rice and stir. Cover the pot and let it simmer for about 18 minutes. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for 5 minutes. Fluff the rice gently with a fork and serve and enjoy!

It's hard to say if you have to do the steps in a specific order without transition words.

Revised paragraph with transition words

Rice is a quick and easy side dish to prepare. First, pour two cups of water into a pot and bring it to a boil. Next, add one cup of rice and stir. After this, cover the pot and let it simmer for about 18 minutes. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for 5 minutes. Finally, fluff the rice gently with a fork and serve and enjoy!

The transition words "first", "next", "after this", and "finally" indicate the order of events. Better not add the rice before boiling the water!
