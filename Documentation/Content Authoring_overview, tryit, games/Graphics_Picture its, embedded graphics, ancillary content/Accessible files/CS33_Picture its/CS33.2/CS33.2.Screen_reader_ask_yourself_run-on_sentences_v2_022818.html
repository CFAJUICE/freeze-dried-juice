Ask yourself: Is the sentence a run-on?

To decide if a sentence is a run-on, ask yourself:

Does it have two or more independent clauses that can stand on their own?

If you answer no, your sentence is not a run-on!

If you answer yes, next ask yourself:

Does it have either a semicolon or a comma and a joining word such as and to join the clauses?

If you answer yes, your sentence is not a run-on!

If you answer no, your sentence is a run-on and needs to be fixed.
