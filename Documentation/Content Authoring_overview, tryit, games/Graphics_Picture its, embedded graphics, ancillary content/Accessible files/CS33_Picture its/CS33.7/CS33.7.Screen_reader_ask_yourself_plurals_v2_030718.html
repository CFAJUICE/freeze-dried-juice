Ask yourself: Deciding if your plurals are correct

To decide if you've used the correct plural version of a noun, ask yourself:

Does my plural include an apostrophe?

If you answer yes, your plurals are not correct.

If you answer no, next ask yourself:

Did I use a dictionary if I'm unsure of the correct plural form?

If you answer no, your plurals are not correct.

If you answer yes, next ask yourself:

For regular nouns, did I add S, E S, or I E S to form the plural?

If you answer no, your plurals are not correct.

If you answer yes, your plurals are correct!
