A compelling introduction

Two people are meeting at a widget- and gadget-making conference and are introducing themselves to each other. Which introduction is more interesting and effective?

Marcia's introduction

Hi! I'm Marcia. My company makes gadgets.

Jose's introduction

Hi, I'm Jose. I'm a product director for Widget Company, the world's largest widget builder. Did you know workers in the US use some kind of widget at least 5 times a day? We think the best way to make a great widget is to talk directly to customers about what they want, build at the highest quality, and provide excellent service.

Which introduction is more effective?

Jose's introduction is more interesting and effective because he starts with a sentence that captures Marcia's interest, provides background information about widgets, and closes with three things to discuss further. This is a compelling introduction.
