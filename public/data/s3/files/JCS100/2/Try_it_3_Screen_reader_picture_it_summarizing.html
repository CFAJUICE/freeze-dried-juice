Summarizing

When you use the main ideas from an original source and use your own words to explain what you mean, you are summarizing. In your summary, you include the main ideas from the original source, but none of the details. Your summary will be considerably shorter than the original.

If you're not an experienced hiker, you might consider buying a package deal from a tour operator. While these packages can be pricey, they save you a lot of time an energy. Many of these tour companies offer lower prices just before the start of, or just after the end of, the prime hiking season. If you're willing to be flexible in your plans, you can save a bundle.
