An effective conclusion

Two people are concluding their conversation after meeting at a widget and gadget-making conference. Which conclusion is more interesting and effective?

Jose's conclusion

Bye. Thanks for talking with me.

Marcia's conclusion

It was great to meet you, Jose. I hope I convinced you that gadgets are the next big thing! Gadgets are making everyday tasks much simpler. The best gadgets are easy to use without any special training. They're fun to use and make even boring tasks more enjoyable. You should go out and buy a gadget today!

Which conclusion is more effective?

Marcia's conclusion is more effective because she restates the points she made earlier and ends with a final thought.
